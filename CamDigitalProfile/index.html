PORTFOLIO

Beyond the Screen





This website, a collaborative effort by Cameron Hammond, Jules Smith, Heather Anderson, and Jimmy Diaz, is the main product we created during our Fall 2023 Integrated Experience in the BYU Information Systems Junior Core. We were awarded 2nd place among 56 groups. The panel of judges was composed of both BYU professors and KPMG employees.

The scenario, given to us by our professors, was to analyze data on social media use and mental health as if we were tasked by Provo city officials. This website showcases our insights on a Tableau dashboard and allows users to contribute to the survey database.

We employed JavaScript and HTML/CSS for the front-end, while the back-end is powered by Node.js. Hosted on AWS via Elastic Beanstalk, the website integrates with a PostgreSQL database managed through AWS RDS.
